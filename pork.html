Gotcha Roasted Pork

Inspired by Soma from Food Wars.

Ingredients

  • 3 packets of bacon
  • 6 potatoes
  • Half a cup of mushrooms
  • 1 small onion
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • quarter cup of red win
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sweet sake
  • Rosemary
  • butcher twine

Steps

  1. Mince onion and mushrooms, preheat oven to 350-370 degrees F
  2. Heat butter in pan and add onions, sauteing until soft and translucent, add mushrooms and saute until fragrant and soft
  3. Wash potatoes and cut them into chunks then steam
  4. Once potatoes are soft, remove and peel skin off
  5. Mash the potatoes then add onions, mushrooms, salt and pepper. mix all ingredients
  6. Form potato mixture into oval shape, layer first set of bacon onto potatos.
  7. Layer the other side and use toothpicks to keep bacon in place while tying the twine
  8. Once completed, add rosemary and then place into oven for 4 hours
  9. While pork is cooking, prepare the sauce by heating up pan on medium heat and adding soy sauce, sake, and wine
  10. Once pork is ready to be take out, serve with sauce and enjoy
